Place your raw nuScenes mini download here.

Expected layout (all under data/nuscenes_raw/):

  maps/              <- from nuScenes mini archive
  samples/           <- from nuScenes mini archive
  sweeps/            <- from nuScenes mini archive
  v1.0-mini/         <- metadata JSONs from nuScenes mini archive
                        MUST also include from LiDARSeg expansion:
                          - lidarseg.json
                          - category.json (overwrites mini version)

  lidarseg/
    v1.0-mini/       <- LiDARSeg .bin label files

Download LiDARSeg expansion: nuScenes-lidarseg-mini-v1.0.tar.bz2
Extract the FULL archive into this folder (not just the .bin files).
The expansion adds lidarseg.json + category.json under v1.0-mini/.

Verify with:
  python -c "from nuscenes.nuscenes import NuScenes; n=NuScenes('v1.0-mini','data/nuscenes_raw',verbose=False); print('lidarseg records:', len(n.lidarseg))"

After files are in place:
  python main.py --convert
